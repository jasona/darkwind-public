Chapter 4: The Sidhe

The Sidhe Fae are demonic faeries of incredible magical aptitude.
Regardless of the information gathered about them in this article
they remain a mysterious enigma. Even the followers of the Ancient
Prince himself do not know what purpose they serve and if they are
even loyal to Set or the Ancient Prince.

The Sidhe have a tight Hierarchal order, much like that of a 
bee society. There is the queen, the Arch-magi, The warriors and
the drones.

The Sidhe queen is rumoured to be a humanoid female of great beauty.
Unlike her fellow Sidhe she's about the same height as a regular
human being. She is unarguably the most powerful of the Sidhe and 
has the voice of a siren. Once she starts to sing any male will stand
paralyzed, listening to her voice, unable to move.

The arch-magi are those individuals who have shown great aptitude
as mages. Trained in the arts of magic since a very young age these 
are the elite troops of the Sidhe.

The warriors are trained in both physical combat as well as the arts
of magic. Their small frail bodies don't appear perfect for physical 
conquest, yet they do rank among the fiercest warriors known to us.

The drones have slight mystical powers and are the working backbone
of the Sidhe colony. Their daily tasks include gathering food and 
maintaining the Sidhe forest.

The Sidhe forest is also known as the forest of no return. According 
to legends this forest exists in three different planes of existence.
Any person walking through the forest will get shifted from plane to 
plane without warning. It is a deadly maze filled with aggressive Sidhe.

According to folklore the Sidhe are unable to conceive children of their
own. Every Sidhe baby is born mutilated and disfigured. During the night
the Sidhe invade the homes of unsuspecting new mothers and replace their
hideous offspring with that of the house owner. The stolen baby will be
transformed into a Sidhe Fae by the Sidhe queen and raised as one of
their own.
