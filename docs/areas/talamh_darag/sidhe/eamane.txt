Commands available:

eheal             - Eamane casts a spell to heal you.
eheal <player>    - Eamane casts a spell to heal another player.
eequip            - Eamane equips all items on her.
eunequip          - Eamane unequips all items on her.
ewear <armor>     - Eamane wears a piece of armor.
eremove <armor>   - Eamane removes a piece of armor.
ewield <weapon>   - Eamane wields a weapon.
eunwield <weapon> - Eamane unwields a weapon.
ereport           - Eamane reports her hp & sp.
