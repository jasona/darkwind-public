                       Serpent's Eye

Serpent's Eye is a medium sized mushroom that is know to have
great stamina increasing powers when grown and processed
correctly.  It has been used for aeons by underworld dwellers
in both religious and medicinal rituals.  It has been called 
by many different names over the years but the most common of
them is Serpent's Eye due to the yellowish designs that resemble
a snake's eye that cover the black head of the mushroom.  These
mushrooms are very delicate in nature and take a long time to
grow.  They require a cool, dark, and damp environment and various
types of nurishment.  They are also highly treasured by trogloditic
tribes which make them very valuable.
