                      Darkhops

Darkhops are one of the major crops raised on Darkwind for use
in the production of alcoholic beverages.  The secret properties
of Darkhops were discovered in the first period following the 
beginning of Darkwind by the wizard cilohocla.  Since its initial
discovery, the inebriation and healing properties of DarkHops 
have been refined through selective breeding of the plants
themselves.The plant stands approximatly 5 feet tall when fully
mature and has a stamen covered with small black folowers emitting
a strong pungent musky aroma.  DarkHops is used exclusively in
the production of alcohol and has no other know use and is of
little nutritional value.  It is a hardy crop resistant to disease
and pests but has a fairly lengthy growing time.  Darkhops prefers
a mild climate with light to moderate humidity as a growing
environment.
