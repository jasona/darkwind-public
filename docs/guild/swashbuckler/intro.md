Swashbucklers
Dashing rogues, gallant courtiers, outlaw kings, adventure-seeking
pirates--these are the faces of the Order of Swashbucklers.  These
diverse warriors are bound by their skill with blades and their
continual search for new lands and new opponents against which to
test their prowess.  From the highest courts to the meanest dockside
taverns, a swashbuckler is always at home, and always ready with a
jest or a blade as they search for fame and fortune.

Swashbucklers are courageous warriors, often going into combat with
only the lightest of armor, trusting to their skill to protect them.
Their training encompasses many weapons, but they particularly excel
with blades--daggers and swords, both common and exotic.  With one--
or two--of these in hand, they utilize their famous blade techniques,
choosing offense or defense as the situation demands.  The most
skilled among them master the infamous *Dance of Death*, which can
decimate opponents.

Combat builds *Flow*, lowering technique costs as the Swashbuckler settles
into rhythm and occasionally granting a guaranteed weapon hit at full Flow.
Swashbuckler Rank comes from GXP and produces the Tier that unlocks abilities.
Dance of Death is a rarer passive expression of full Flow, not a toggled
technique.

## Joining

If you wish to learn more about the Swashbucklers, visit their
guild and speak to the blademistress.  The guild is located near
the docks of Darkwind, 3 east, south, west from the center of town.
There is also a Kerei guild branch, the Kensai, located near the docks,
just outside of Odako's fortress.  In Souvrael, you can visit the
Desert Legion headquarters just south of Souvrael City, and in
Hyperborea, we maintain a small cave near the docks as a base for
our activities.
