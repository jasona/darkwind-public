Offensive Spin - Guild Level 4

The offensive spin is another early blade technique to be
learned. The swashbuckler uses his manual dexterity to spin
his weapons around his body, striking unpredictably. As a
result, his swings become difficult to defend against, and
will land more often upon his opponent.

SP:  5 SP (round)

*Effects(s)*: 15% increased hit chance
