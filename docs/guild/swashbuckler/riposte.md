Riposte - Guild Level 7

Riposte is a mid-level blade technique. The swashbuckler, by
anticipating an attack is able to respond to an incoming
blow from a third party (someone he's not attacking) with a
quick strike of his own. Note that parrying is not necessary
to execute ripostes.

SP:  5 SP (proc)

*Effects(s)*: Chance to redirect an attack from a secondary opponent
