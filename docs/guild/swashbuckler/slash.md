Slash - Guild Level 2

Slashing can often be an effective, if somewhat crude way
of attacking an opponent. Using brute strength alone, a
swashbuckler attempts to make direct, powerful attacks
against his opponent. While the attacks are no more
likely to hit than usual, blows that land cause greater
damage.

SP:  2 SP (proc)

*Effects(s)*: Chance of bracket-scaled damage on hit: +6 below Hero, +7 at
Hero, +9 at Legend, +10 at Epic, and +12 at Mythic.
