Finesse

Finesse is a measure of a Swashbuckler's combative abilities. As
a Swashbuckler becomes more skilled, they will learn to fight with
more finesse. Finesse is distinct from Flow: Finesse limits which styles can
be active together, while Flow discounts their triggered SP costs. Different
fighting styles will require more or less finesse depending on their
difficulty. More about these finesse
requirements can be found in *'sbskills'*.

A Swashbuckler starts with very little finesse and gains 
additional points through higher amounts of Guild Level, dexterity,
intelligence, and charisma. Rank-derived Tier synchronizes the legacy guild
level used by this calculation. They can then run up to or equal to that
finesse in different styles.
