Slip - Guild Level 3

A Swashbuckler can spend a little Flair to slip inside an
opponent's guard and land a quick weapon strike.

Slip costs no spell points. It requires a slashing or
piercing weapon in the main hand.

Cost: 2 Flair

Command: `slip <target>` (defaults to opponent if in combat)

Timer: micro damage timer

See also: flair
