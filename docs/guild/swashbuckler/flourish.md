Flourish - Guild Level 13

The penultimate blade technique, flourish represents the
full realization of a swashbuckler's blade training. The
swashbuckler executing this technique twirls his weapon(s)
in an intricate, beautiful, and deadly series of flourishes.
A swashbuckler executing this technique mesmerizes his
opponent with his skill, enabling the swashbuckler to land
his blows more easily and more powerfully. Rather than
relying on speed, this technique's effectiveness is based
on the swashbuckler's ability to charm and entrance his
opponent.

SP:  3 SP (round)

*Effects(s)*: Improves other techniques as well as abilities
