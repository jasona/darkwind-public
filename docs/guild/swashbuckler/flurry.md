Flurry - Guild Level 12

Speed is the swashbucklers' greatest advantage in combat,
and nowhere is that more obvious than the flurry skill. By
concentrating on the offensive, a swashbuckler can take
advantage of the slightest openings and strike at his enemy
more often.

Every successful Flurry proc guarantees one extra swing. At Hero level, it
can gain a second extra swing; at Legend level, it can gain a third. Raw
Swashbuckler Rank is the dominant part of both chances, while character level
adds a smaller bonus. The second swing chance caps at 85%; the third caps at
50% and is rolled only when the second extra swing succeeds.

SP:  8 SP (proc, reduced by Flow)

*Effects(s)*: Extra swing(s)
