Dance of Death - Passive Flow Technique (Tier 15)

The Dance of Death, the kensai's legendary '*whirlwind attack*',
is the swashbuckler's ultimate technique. A
synthesis of mind, body, and blade which is deceptively
delicate and lethally beautiful. It combines the aspects
of many of the swashbuckler's individual techniques to
create a cocoon of steel round the warrior.

Dance is no longer started or stopped. Combat builds Flow automatically.
At 100 Flow, each eligible combat round has a 5% chance to steal one
guaranteed weapon hit. At Tier 15, full Flow also has a separate, very rare
chance to execute the Dance message and make the next paid technique that
triggers cost no SP. The token ends with combat if unused.

Commands: *starttech sdance* and *stoptech sdance* explain the passive rule.
