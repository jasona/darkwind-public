Spectral Siphon - Tier 18 Technique

Use *starttech siphon* to let the Order's spectral blades drink magical
strength from enemies you hit. A trigger costs 6 SP before its Flow discount,
drains up to 12 SP from the target, and restores exactly what was drained.
An empty target still consumes the triggered technique's cost.

Finesse: 4
Trigger chance: 20%, improved by Flourish
