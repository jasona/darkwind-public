Weaponbreak - Guild Level 9

A practised swashbuckler can catch an enemy's weapon on his
blade and, with a sharp twist, break it, rendering the enemy
much weaker. This skill is more intricate and difficult to
learn than disarm, but can also be more effective at
weakening your enemy. Weapons broken in this manner are
considerably reduced in effectiveness

SP:  40 SP (proc)

*Effects(s)*: Chance to break an opponent's weapon
