Press - Guild Level 8

A Swashbuckler can spend most of their Flair to press a
controlled current opponent.

Your own active Challenge remains the strongest opening and grants a full
immediate attack round. Paralysis, freezing, or restraint of both arms and
legs grants one main-weapon attack sequence. Blindness, loss of
concentration, chilled, a movement lock, or lesser entanglement grants one
main-weapon swing and leaves a surviving foe Exposed for four seconds.
Silence and damage-over-time effects are not openings.

Press costs no spell points and has no timer. Every version costs the same
8 Flair and requires the target to be your current opponent.

Cost: 8 Flair

Command: `press <target>` (defaults to opponent if in combat)

See also: flair, challenge, status effects
