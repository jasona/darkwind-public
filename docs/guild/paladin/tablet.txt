
|YK0                Paladin code of conduct

|YK01:  Obey the knights of higher station in all things.

|YK02:  Value the honour of our order above our personal honour,
|YK0    value our order above our lives.

|YK03:  Render aid to those in need as you would a fellow knight.
|YK0    Indulge the ignorant or witless even unto fault, but
|YK0    suffer no slur or insult to our honour from those who
|YK0    lack such excuse.

|YK04:  Never violate an oath you swear. Never swear an oath
|YK0    you can not keep.

|YK05:  Engage the enemy forthrightly that he may know the
|YK0    origin of his doom, set no ambush nor indulge in 
|YK0    trickery to overcome a foe.

|YK06:  Give quarter to those who yield honorably.
 
|YK07:  Show great courage and bravery, but value your wisdom
|YK0    above all else.

|YK08:  Refuse no honourable truce or parley.

|YK09:  Do not break the laws of the realm thus to bring about
|YK0    the wrath of those who make this place run.

|YK010: Suffer not to shelter among us any who would violate, 
|YK0    ignore, or dishonour our Code.
